Web Design + Development

Clean and Simple

The hallmark of my work is simplicity. I know how frustrating it is to arrive at a web page and immediately feel lost or confused about what to do or where to go. That's why I've made it my mission in life to create user experiences that allow technology to work for the user—not against them.

Optimized Performance

Computers and Internet access are faster than they have ever been before—but users are also more impatient than ever before! Fortunately, there are great tools available to developers to ensure that content is delivered in a concise manner, and only as needed.

Responsive Design

I started my first responsive design project at Boeing in 2011, and I've never looked back. With such a wide range of devices that people can use to visit your site, it's crucial to provide them with a great experience regardless of their screen size or device type.

User-Centered Approach

I worked with the Usability Services group at Boeing in 2010 and discovered through hands-on experience how crucial it is to make users the focal point of design. The mission of a design and development team should be to create useful tools that make life easier for users.
